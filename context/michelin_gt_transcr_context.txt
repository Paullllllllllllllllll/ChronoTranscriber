Michelin Red Guide pages. Scanned restaurant and hotel
listings from seven European country series (France,
Germany, Britain, Italy, Benelux, Switzerland, Portugal
and Spain), published 1976-2023. Each page contains
multiple entries with establishment names, addresses,
telephone numbers, pricing, and standardized symbols.

Key symbols and their printed appearance:

Stars (rosette flowers): 1-3 consecutive flower-shaped
rosettes indicate Michelin star awards.
Bib Gourmand: a round smiling face (the Michelin Man).
Cutlery rating: 1-5 crossed fork-and-spoon pairs for
restaurant comfort class.
Hotel class: 1-5 small house/pavilion icons.
Pleasant marker: a heart or flower icon.
Green star: a leaf or clover symbol (post-2020).
New in guide: "N" or "NEW" in a box.
Telephone: a handset icon preceding phone numbers.
Postal: an envelope icon preceding postal codes.
Parking: a "P" symbol.
Credit cards: text abbreviations AE, VISA, JCB, E.
